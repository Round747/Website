Kat⁵

a comic about emotions

About

This comic is about a woman named Katherine, who has the ability to seperate her emotions from herself, who then take on physical forms. With the ability for emotions to have conversations with themselves and other people, I use this to write personal sotries about feelings, and how you should never try to shun them, even if you perceive them as bad.
As a note, these chapters are designed to be episodic. Theoretically, you can view them in any order, but I write them assuming the reader has read the previous ones.
