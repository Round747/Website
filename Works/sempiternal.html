semPiTernal

Short narrative game made for Brackeys Game Jam 2025.1

February 23, 2025
[This game is only playable on desktop]



Play Game

Controls:
Move - WASD or Arrow keys
Interact - Left mouse click
Menu - Escape

Sempiternal is a short story game I created for Brackeys Game Jam 2025.1. You can read about it and play it for free from Itch.io.
After the first week, I spent another week after the jam finishing up the game. Aside from some better gameplay, no major changes were made.

For the jam, my game received 27 votes and an overall placement of 262nd out of 2140.
Ratings were out of 5 stars.
