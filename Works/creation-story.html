creation story

set in the Wardenbearers universe

March 4, 2024

IN THE BEGINNING, the land was desolate and devoid of atmosphere. It was visited by a group of people. These people saw the world not for what it was, but for what it could be. These were the first New Worlders.
FIRST, they created the world’s new moon. A magnet, which protected the New World from the harmful rays of the sun. In the creation of this device, many New Worlders lost their lives.
NEXT, they delivered the world oxygen, filling the valley with atmosphere. Bringing clouds, rain, and thunderstorms. In the creation of this atmosphere, many New Worlders lost their lives.
NEXT, the New Worlders brought water to the land, creating rivers, lakes, and waterfalls. In the delivering of this water, many New Worlders lost their lives.
THEN, the people finally set foot on the world they had created. They breathed in the air they had made, they bathed in the water they had brought, they felt the warmth of the sun’s rays.
FINALLY, They brought forth those who gave their lives for their dream. All their bodies were buried under the soil. Giving life to the first trees planted on the New World.
