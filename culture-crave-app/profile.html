Aisha Rahman, a 24-year-old freelance graphic designer, is walking through Parramatta Square on a Saturday afternoon after finishing a meeting at a local café.

She is looking for something creative to do for the rest of her weekend and recalls seeing a friend’s Instagram story tagged with #CultureCrave. She taps the link, which leads her to a short reel and a download prompt for the app.

She installs Culture Crave from the Google Play Store and opens it. The interface is clean and intuitive, offering all available locations and a pop-up map. She enables location access and finds the ‘Bare Skills’ workshop just two blocks away. After reading the information and tapping the directions she’s guided to the venue via Google Maps.

Inspired by the experience, she shares it on social media, bookmarks upcoming events, and feels more connected to Parramatta’s creative community.
