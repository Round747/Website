About

This series currently has two seperate yet similar universes. In both, the story follows Daisy Parker as she solves crimes and woos women.
My first series, "The Adventures of Daisy Parker", follows her during the Great War, as she is inducted into the military police. Its main inspiration is "The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes".
My second series, which I plan to make, follows another Daisy in a different universe where, after the war, strange creatures have begun emerging from the deep. Its main inspiration is "The X Files".
