AbouT

I am a very creative person with a passion for digital art, creative writing, and reading. I enjoy making comics, writing short stories, and like to experiment with all sorts of other genres, styles and mediums.

I also like coding, with projects such as games and websites. For personal projects, I place great emphasis on the storytelling elements, and like incorporating the medium into the delivery of the story.

In my free time (when I'm not writing or drawing) I watch TV shows and movies for the enjoyment and to also dissect how they work and what makes them an effective piece of storytelling and art.

I love old and retro technology, mainly that from the 1980s. I like to find old computers or cameras, and take them apart to understand how they work, and fix them. I really like retro and 80s aesthetics and incorporate them in my art and stories.

I have been writing since 2020, but my best work, and that which is on this website, is from 2022 onwards. Click the button below to head to my Works page.
